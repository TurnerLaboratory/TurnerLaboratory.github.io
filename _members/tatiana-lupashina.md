Tatiana holds a Master of Science in Medical Neurosciences from Charité Universitätsmedizin Berlin, where she specialized in multisensory processing and animal behavior. She is particularly interested in exploring the visual system of the fruit fly, working to understand how neural circuits give rise to behavior.
Alongside her research, she is committed to scientific communication, translating complex concepts for diverse audiences through transdisciplinary methods and collaborative efforts that expand scientific accessibility. Outside the lab, she creates interactive and immersive art experiences with EDGE Neuroscience & Art e.V..
